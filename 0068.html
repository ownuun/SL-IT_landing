FAXI

Problem

The shift from analog to digital communication has led to the disappearance of meaningful message exchanges. The depth and personal touch of physical messages are lost, making everyday interactions feel less significant and less impactful.

Solution

FAXI reintroduces meaningful communication by offering a platform that blends the efficiency of digital with the personal touch of analog by using a "mini fax". Our product makes the exchange of messages carry weight and foster deeper connections, making everyday life more meaningful through thoughtful and impactful interactions.

TREEDI

Problem

Learning complex topics in a linear chat is inefficient. When users ask a "drill-down" question, the new answer contaminates the main context. This forces the user to constantly scroll back and forth to regain the high-level picture, which is cumbersome and breaks their flow of thought.

Solution

Treedi solves this by transforming AI chats into an interactive knowledge tree. Our node-based interface lets you non-destructively "branch out" to explore sub-topics without ever losing the main context. This "bird's-eye view" of your entire thought process makes complex learning intuitive and efficient.
